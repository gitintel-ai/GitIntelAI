# Influencer & Distribution Outreach Plan

## Strategy: Discovery, Not Cold Pitching

Do NOT cold-DM anyone with "check out my tool." Instead:

1. Publish the research blog post ("I analyzed 50 repos...")
2. Tag relevant people when sharing chart screenshots on X
3. Submit to newsletters via their official submission forms
4. Engage authentically in their communities before and after launch
5. Let the data do the selling -- if the research is good, people share it

## What NOT to Do

- Do not cold-DM anyone with "hey check out my tool"
- Do not spam the same link in multiple Discord servers
- Do not ask for retweets
- Do not fabricate endorsements or usage numbers
- Do not tag more than 3 people per tweet
- Let the research data be the hook, not the product
